## The Traffic Buffet: Pick Your Format

PPCmate supports five main advertising formats, each with its own pricing model:

**Pop-under V2 Ads (CPM)** - The classic. New window, full attention. Some people hate them, but they work for certain verticals. Starting at $0.10 CPM.

**Push Notifications (CPC)** - Those little alerts that appear on people's devices. Minimum cost per click at $0.001, though they recommend bidding between $0.02 and $0.05 for decent results. This is where PPCmate seems to shine according to user reviews.

**Display Ads (CPM)** - Your standard banner ads. Responsive sizing, strategic placements, nothing revolutionary but solid.

**Native Ads (CPM)** - Blends into content, less intrusive. Better for brand-building than hard sells.

**Video Ads (CPM)** - For when you've got compelling video content and want eyeballs on it.

The platform also offers CPA optimization for CPM campaigns, which is basically the algorithm trying to get you actual conversions instead of just clicks and impressions.
